# App Review Notes Patterns

## General App Review Notes

Thank you for reviewing {AppName}.

{AppName} is designed to {short purpose}. The app does not require account registration unless explicitly stated in the app flow.

Privacy Policy:
{privacy_url}

Terms of Use:
{terms_url}

## Photos Permission App

The app requests Photos access only so users can select or manage user-chosen photos. Photos are processed locally on the device unless the product explicitly states otherwise.

## Camera Permission App

The app requests Camera access only for user-initiated capture features. Camera content is processed according to the app's stated functionality.

## Face Data / Face Detection App

Face detection, if used, is limited to temporary on-device detection results such as bounding boxes. The app does not use this data for identification, biometric authentication, tracking, advertising, profiling, or model training unless explicitly disclosed and implemented.

## Subscription App

The app uses Apple In-App Purchase for subscription access. Include subscription title, duration, price, auto-renewal behavior, privacy policy URL, and Terms/EULA URL in metadata where appropriate.

## Local Processing / No Backend App

All core processing is performed locally on the device. The app does not upload user content to a backend server for advertising, tracking, or model training.

## Demo Material Notice

App Store screenshots and preview materials use sample, generated, or non-personal demo content. They do not represent private user data.

## AI-generated screenshot material notice

If screenshots include generated faces/images, state that the App Store screenshots and preview videos use AI-generated sample materials and do not depict real persons.
